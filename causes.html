Cause 1

Sunway Group’s four pillars of sustainability are outlined based on the 17 goals under the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (UN-SDG). This SDG focuses on developing and expanding renewable energy resources such as sun, wind, hydropower, liquid and solid biofuels, biogas and geothermal. These renewable sources of energy do not emit greenhouse gasses into the atmosphere, and are therefore ideal for the environment and human health.
